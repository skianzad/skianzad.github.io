Cricket

Vibroacoustic therapy device

Cricket is a handheld vibroacoustic therapy (VAT) device engineered to deliver natural, low-frequency vibrations through the body. Connect with nature through quality signals inspired by the soothing resonance of a Tibetan singing bowl and gentle cricket symphonies — designed to support relaxation, stress relief, and wellbeing.

Our state-of-the-art haptic technology uses precise actuator placement to create effective VAT experiences. The device features an ergonomic, pebble-like form with a matte white body and refined metallic accents, built for comfort and daily use.

Key features

  • Low-frequency VAT signals (20–250 Hz) aligned with body’s energy channels
  • Ergonomic, handheld design with intuitive central control
  • Pair with the Sensci app to access VAT signal libraries and personalize signals
  • Evidence-informed design for relaxation, pain relief, and stress reduction
  • Premium build: matte white finish with polished metallic trim

How it works

Vibroacoustic therapy delivers mechanical vibrations through the skin and tissues. Cricket generates these signals using carefully tuned actuators, so you can experience the benefits of VAT at home or on the go. Use the Sensci app to browse, customize, and save your preferred signals — from nature-inspired presets to your own combinations.
