Advanced Virtual Keyboard & Trackpad for Foldable PCs

Led research, design, and system validation of Huawei's first production virtual keyboard + trackpad for the MateBook Fold 非凡大师 (Ultimate Design), an 18" HarmonyOS foldable PC that can switch between a full-screen canvas and a laptop mode with on-display keyboard and trackpad.

When folded, a light touch of eight fingers brings up the virtual keyboard so you can type ideas at any time. Multiple skins are available, and key spacing and layout are adjustable for a personalized yet efficient typing experience; the built-in linear motor provides a natural and realistic typing feel.

Under the hood, I developed the sensing and modeling stack for this experience: Gaussian touch‑offset models plus language models to speed up blind typing on the flexible surface, multimodal state machines to keep pointing precise, and deep learning models that suppress accidental touches and palm contacts.

Client Huawei Technologies · 2012 Labs

CourtReady: AI-Assisted Legal Guidance Platform

Co-founded CourtReady.ca, a legal tech platform providing free tools and accessible legal education for Canadians who cannot easily afford counsel. Designed a hybrid RAG-based system that powers limitation-period calculators, flight disruption tools, and small-claims workflows, with content surfaced through CourtReady Academy. Built chunking + embedding pipelines and improved retrieval accuracy using hybrid HyDE strategies so non-lawyers can answer "what do I do next?" in plain language.
